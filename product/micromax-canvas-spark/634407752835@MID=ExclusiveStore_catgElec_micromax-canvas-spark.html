It offers an incredible 540 x 960 screen resolution along with 16 M colour depth for enhanced clarity and details. Additionally, the display is safeguarded by the advanced Corning Gorilla Glass 3 material that not only makes it extremely durable but also prevents it from unwanted scratches. 

      8 MP Primary Camera and 2 MP Front Facing Camera

      The Micromax Canvas Spark Q380 comes with an advanced 8 MP Autofocus enabled primary camera that helps you to capture every moment with immense clarity and optimum colour balance. Equipped with the technologically upgraded OV Sensor 8858 along with Largan Lens, this smartphone will truly expand your photography skills to the utmost. It also offers LED Flash support that allows you to capture flawless pictures even in low light shooting situations. The 2 MP front facing camera of the Canvas Spark Q380 smartphone is a pure delight for selfie lovers. It also connects you to your loved ones via crystal clear video calls.

      Dual SIM Support

      Get the freedom to choose your favourite mobile network and always stay connected with the best of network connectivity with the dual SIM support feature of the Micromax smartphone. Additionally, it also helps you to keep two different numbers for your work and professional life.

      1.3 GHz Quad Core Cortex A7 Processor

      Wait less and do more with the Micromax Canvas Spark Q380 smartphone that comes with the latest 1.3 GHz Quad Core, Cortex A7 processor. This excellent processor delivers a lag free performance and enables you to easily navigate in between apps without any hassle.  1 GB of RAM further maximizes the processing speed of the smartphone and amazes you with a superfast and responsive navigation experience.

      Android 5.0 Lollipop Operating System (OS)

      Powered by the user-friendly Android 5.0 Lollipop Operating System (OS), the Micromax Canvas Spark Q380 smartphone thrills you with an incredible operating speed. With this excellent operating system (OS) you can always keep a check on the important notifications directly on the lock screen. It facilitates you with a priority mode in which you can customize the app usage while the Pin Apps option enables you to limit the access of the app for other users.

      3G and Wi-Fi Enabled

      Designed to cater your active lifestyle requirements, the Micromax Canvas Spark Q380 smartphone offers both 3G and Wi-Fi connectivity. Simply, connect the smartphone with the available Wi-Fi network and enjoy superfast internet connectivity. You can share your pictures on Facebook, download movies, listen to music and browse web through the Wi-Fi connectivity of the smartphone. Moreover, by using a 3G data card you can avail blazing fast internet connection on the go. Additionally, the Bluetooth connectivity of the Micromax smartphone allows you to share your data instantly without any cable clutters.

      Excellent Storage Capacity

      With an incredible 8 GB ROM, the Micromax Canvas Spark Q380 smartphone allows you to store almost everything in the smartphone without facing any space crunch. Carry your favourite videos, pictures, songs, files and more wherever you go with the Canvas smartphone and access it whenever you wish. Moreover, if you still require some additional space in the smartphone you can use a microSD card with 32 GB capacity.

      Astounding Battery Performance

      Stay hooked up with your loved ones 24 x 7 without any interruption with the Micromax Canvas Spark Q380 smartphone. Equipped with a long-lasting 2000 mAh battery, this high performance smartphone delivers an outstanding 335 hours standby time and 7 hours of talk time. You can enjoy your long conversations, play entertaining games online, listen to music and browse web without running out of juice.
