      Description

      Lenovo A7000 is a budget smartphone that comes with advanced features. It has a large 13.97 cm (5.5) IPS HD screen. With its 8 MP primary camera and 5 MP front camera, you can click beautiful images. The 1.5 GHz octa-core processor and 2 GB RAM provides swift navigation and processing experience. You can insert two SIM cards in this device. It is 3G, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth enabled. This smartphone offers a long lasting battery performance. Buy it from Snapdeal at best possible price with 1 year warranty.

      13.97 cm (5.5) IPS HD Display Screen

      To enhance your gaming and viewing experience, Lenovo A7000 Smartphone incorporates a large 13.97 cm (5.5) High Definition display screen. It makes you navigate through different applications easily and offers a rich entertaining experience. This IPS display produces vivid colours and brilliant contrast which is perfect for recording videos. It lends 16 million colours and produces resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels with 5-point multitouch.

      ARM Mali760 MP2 1.5 GHz Octa Core Processor & 2GB RAM

      This Lenovo smartphone features a responsive ARM Mali760 MP2 octa-core processor that delivers power-packed performance. Having 2 GB RAM and 1.5 GHz clock-speed, A7000 allows you to play games, view videos, surf internet and do a lot more seamlessly. You won’t face any time-lag issues while moving from one application to another.

      8 MP Auto Focus Rear Camera with Dual LED Flash

      Click high resolution images with superb clarity and sharpness with 8 MP auto-focus rear camera of Lenovo A7000 smartphone. It comes with dual LED flash camera that produces brilliant quality photos. It lends resolution of 3264 x 2448 pixels. It offers features like face detection, geo-tagging and touch focus. You can also record videos using the primary camera.

      5 MP Front Camera

      For all the selfie lovers, there is front camera with 5 megapixel using which you can capture stunning self-portraits. You can also use this front camera for doing video chat with your friends.

      Android Lollipop 5.0 OS

      Lenovo A7000 smartphone is equipped with an advanced Android version. Lollipop 5.0 operating system lets you access thousands of apps and surf internet seamlessly. It helps in prioritising your contact list and personalising your notifications in a better way. This OS also saves your battery providing long lasting performance.

      Dual SIM Phone

      You can insert two different SIM cards in this Lenovo smartphone. It enables you to take advantage of two different service providers. You can also use dual SIM technology to keep your professional and personal life separate. While travelling to a different country, this dual slim slot comes handy in connecting you to both local and global networks simultaneously.

      8 GB Internal Memory Expandable up to 32 GB

      Lenovo A7000 smartphone comes with 8 GB ROM. You can insert a micro SD card to expand the memory of this smart phone up to 32 GB. Storing a large amount of images, videos, data files and more get easier with expandable memory of this Lenovo smart phone.

      Connectivity Options

      This Lenovo smartphone is 4G enabled. You can connect it with available Wi-Fi networks in the area for performing high speed downloads. This phone has GPS technology that allows you to access location maps. It lets you pair with nearby smart devices for sharing files via Bluetooth. You can tune into FM radio and listen to songs on this mobile.

      2900 mAh Battery

      Equipped with Li-Po 2900 mAh battery, Lenovo A7000 gives you 16 hours of talk time and 264 hours of standby on 2G and 3G network. On 2G network, it delivers 39 hours of talk time.
