      11.43 cm (4.5) IPS Display

      Treat your eyes with rich visuals and enhanced graphics with the bright 11.43 cm (4.5) IPS display of the Xolo Prime smartphone. It showcases every image with supreme clarity and details at 218 PPI. Moreover, the advanced in-plane switching (IPS) display technology of the smartphone renders true-to-life colours for a more comfortable viewing experience without straining your eyes.

       

       

       

      1.3 GHz Quad Core Processor

      Do you love browsing web but hate waiting? Now, enjoy smooth multitasking experience with the new Xolo smartphone that incorporates the latest 1.3 GHz Quad Core Processor. Additionally, 1 GB of RAM speeds up the processing power and helps you to easily navigate in between apps without any hassle.

       

      Android 5.0 Lollipop Operating System (OS)

      The Xolo smartphone comes with the sweetness of Android 5.0 lollipop operating system. It helps you to download innumerable apps in the phone that can easily sort out your everyday tasks in a jiffy. It gives you an instant access to the files and apps stored in the smartphone and also deliver a prioritised list of contacts based on your frequent calls.

       

       

      5 MP AF Primary Camera and Front VGA Camera

      Capture those beautiful moments forever with the 5 MP Autofocus enabled primary camera of the Xolo Prime smartphone. It enables you to capture blur-free images infused with optimum colour balance and sharp details. You can also record high quality videos with this phone. With the front VGA camera of the device you can capture stunning selfies with more natural colour tones and no noise.

       

       

      Live Bright With Prime

      Beautifully designed, XOLO Prime is available in four exciting colors of black, Blue Red and Gold along with a Glass Finish back. Color your world with prime and pick the one that best suits your personality.

       

       

       

      8 GB ROM and 32 GB Expandable Memory

      Carry your important documents with you wherever you go with the Xolo Prime smartphone. It offers an excellent 8 GB of ROM for storing anything you like. Store your videos, songs, images, files and more in the Xolo smartphone without facing any space crunch. Moreover, if you require some additional storage space in the phone you can use a microSD card with 32 GB storage capacity.

       

      1800 mAh Battery

      Much acclaimed for its unparalleled performance, the Xolo Prime smartphone incorporates a powerful 1800 mAh battery. The astounding battery performance of the smartphone enables you to easily accomplish your important tasks without worrying about the battery life. From your long conversations to an unlimited web browsing session, the Xolo smartphone always keeps you entertained without a pause. It delivers a talk time of up to 10 hours on 3G and standby time of 400 hours.
