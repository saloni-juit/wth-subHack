      Description

      Overview

      Experience the most engaging smartphone features at an affordable price with Micromax Canvas Spark 2. This dual SIM smartphone comes with a powerful 1.3 GHz Quad-core processor along with the latest Android Lollipop 5.1 Operating System (OS). It enables you to watch movies, play game, listen to music, download your favourite apps and explore web at blazing fast speed with its superfast 3G connectivity. Canvas Spark 2 packs all the latest features required to keep you on the go. You can buy Micromax Canvas Spark 2 at best price along with 1 year Micromax India warranty only at Snapdeal.     

      12.7 cm (5) IPS Screen

      How about playing your favourite games on the large 12.7 cm (5) IPS screen? Micromax Canvas Spark 2 enhances your viewing experience at 854 x 480 screen resolution and 16.7 M colour depth. The IPS display technology gives you the best view for an enthralling viewing experience.

       

      Superfast Processor

      Equipped with a powerful 1.3 GHz Quad-core processor, Canvas Spark 2 ensures seamless multitasking experience. It enables you to work on multiple tabs simultaneously without any hassle. 768 MB of RAM ensures smooth data transition for a superfast navigation experience like never before.

       

       

      Android Lollipop 5.1 Operating System (OS)

      Power packed with the sweetest Android Lollipop 5.1 operating system (OS), Canvas Spark 2 amazes you with exciting new features such as stylish design, pin apps, priority mode, notification on lock screen and much more. With this user-friendly operating system (OS), you can download many entertaining apps in the smartphone to make things fast and easy.

       

      Excellent Camera

      Click beautiful moments of life forever with the Canvas Spark 2 smartphone. It comes with an advanced 5 MP FF rear camera with LED flash that enables you to click bright and well-illuminated pictures even in low light. It also has a 2 MP FF camera that is great for clicking selfies and making video calls. Moreover, it also offers advance camera features such as face detection mode.

      3G Connectivity

      Maximize your productivity to the utmost with the Micromax Canvas Spark 2. You can share your important files, send emails, download movies, enjoy music and explore web at superfast speed with the 3G connectivity. It also offers other connectivity options such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, USB port and much more.

      FirsTouch Launcher

      Make those everyday tasks simple and easy with the First Touch Menu:

      ·         Swipe right to translate, or left to transliterate

      ·         First Touch menu - to easily access your favourite apps

      ·         Regional keyboard – to send quick personalised messages

      ·         App bazaar - download apps in your regional languages

                                

      Expandable Memory

      Keep all your multimedia files in the Micromax Canvas Spark 2 and access it from anywhere you wish. With 4 GB ROM (1.73GB (Apps + Phone storage), this smartphone enables you to store videos, images, songs, games, files and much more. Moreover, by using a microSD card you can expand the memory of the device up to 32 GB.

       

      Amazing Battery Performance

      Loaded with a powerful 1800 mAh battery, Micromax Canvas Spark 2 entertains you for a longer time without running out of juice.  Access your mailbox, download movies, work on your business presentations and do anything that you love with Canvas Spark 2 without worrying about the battery getting drained out. It delivers a standby time of 324 hours and talk time of 6 hours 30 minutes.

                              

      About Micromax

      Micromax is an Indian consumer electronics company with its headquarters in Gurgaon (India). It started selling mobile phones in the year 2008 and has become one of the largest domestic mobile handset companies operating in low cost feature phone segments by 2010. Additionally, Micromax became the first Indian mobile phone company to start selling its products in Russia.
