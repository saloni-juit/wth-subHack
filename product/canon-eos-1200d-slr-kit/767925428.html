      Description

      Photography is certainly an art and so is the choice of a right camera! In this scenario, when compact cameras are striving hard to make their presence felt in the smartphones generation, DSLRs have their own charm and yes, a strong market though. With the rising interest of novice photographers, DSLRs are gaining huge popularity. Undoubtedly, brands like Pentax and Nikon are among the topmost choices of experienced as well as beginners, Canon has made a remarkable effort to enter this DSLR arena with its new and powerful EOS 1200D Kit (EF S18-55 IS II). Packed with a host of smart features and excellent filters, this latest entry from Canon perfectly caters to the needs and requirements of first time users. It allows the novice photographers to capture sharp images along with super-fine visual clarity.

      Canon EOS 1200D is definitely a lucrative prospect for novice photographers and what makes it more attractive is the follow-up launch of the user-friendly EOS Companion app, which is meant for helping the 1200D users. When compared to Canon 1100D, this 1200D takes away all the points with its 18MP sensor that gives a remarkable resolution jump from that 12.2MP of Canon 1100D. Other than this, the DSLR comes loaded with smart features such as:

      Improved ISO Range

      Now you can enjoy clicking in day and night as Canon 1200D Kit (EF S18-55 IS II) comes with a wide ISO range of 100 to 6400, which you can even expand up to 12,800. This DSLR model offers its users to select their choice of expressions either in dark rooms or in bright daylight. Ranging from low sensitivity coverage to high sensitivity, 1200D enables you to click blur-free and high resolution photos.

      Tcellent Scene Intelligent Auto Mode

      Featuring the Scene Intelligent Auto mode, Canon EOS 1200D Kit (EF S18-55 IS II) takes the Perfect photography concept a level up. As soon as you select the Scene Intelligent Auto mode, the DSLR goes on complete auto-pilot mode in which it adjusts itself to the optimal settings mode. Depending upon the factors like contrast, colour, distance and brightness, the camera is capable to recognize the scene that would be shot. Whether its nature photography, an evening shot, portrait photography or shots with extreme bright backgrounds, the EOS 1200D Kit (EF S18-55 IS II) is capable enough to recognize all kinds of scenes.

       Go Creative

      Canon 1200D comes with five creative filters that would surely add the right mood to your photos and videos. The creative filters of this DSLR include and Fish-eye effect, Soft focus effect, Grainy Black and White, Miniature effect and Toy camera effect.

      Superb Images

      Canon EOS 1200D is provided with a single-lens reflex, digital, AF/AE camera along with built-in flash. Its CMOS image sensor is around 22.3x14.9 mm and it can capture images up to 18MP along with an aspect ratio of 3:2. Also, thanks to its dust delete feature that provides clear and dust-free photographs.
