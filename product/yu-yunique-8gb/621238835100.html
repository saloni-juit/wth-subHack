      I Suggest All Prospective Buyers Read This...
      I've been using this phone for a week now and I'd like to share some important things for anyone planning to buy it. (I wish I had this info beforehand) First, the pros; Display is crisp and visibility in direct sunlight is decent. Camera IMO isn't as bad as reviews make it out to be but that's subjective. design is 5stars, slim and light. on-screen buttons give a nice premium look. call quality is good so far. I don't game but I'm happy with it's performance, no lags so far. 2000mAh battery is exactly that. don't go expecting miracles from it. it lasts me 7-8hrs of fairly heavy usage (lots of calls, 4G on, messaging, pulling up apps, web, etc) from a full charge, i have no complaints on that front so far. Now the Cons (IMPORTANT!!!) 1. Privacy apps like app lockers, call/sms hiders, etc, don't work well. This is documented even on Yu forums, it's a bug 2. If you install a 3rd party launcher like Apex launcher or buzz launcher, etc., on reboot it goes back to stock google launcher. ie, you have to set your launcher every time you reboot the phone. Another documented bug. 3. the phone sometimes will freeze, the screen goes blank and buttons are unresponsive. you cannot remove the battery to reset it so you have to get someone to call you and hope it responds. another documented bug. 4. Don't cry for Cyanogen Mod...I was told point blank by Yu support by email (support@yuplaygod.com) that since this phone came with android, there will be no Cyanogen update for it. Don't bother calling their call centre, they have no idea about anything and read from scripts. 5. Back cover is pretty flimsy. I know I'll have to change it soon. It's also a finger print magnet but i don't really mind that. 6. playing music/calls on earphones, ANY earphones, is hit and miss. music will randomly stop and the player stays on the notification drop-down even when the player is closed. call cannot be ended from the earphone button unless you do so from the phone. All in all, it's a decent phone for it's price, although very very buggy, and no cyanogen mod despite them advertising it as an option. I get the feeling they rushed lollipop 5.1.1 because COS either wasn't ready or micromax and cyanogen are fighting amongst themselfs as was reported. either way, one week after buying this, I wish I knew what I know now. I would have gone for the Dazen 1 instead. More RAM, better camera and a bug free kitkat experience at the same price.
