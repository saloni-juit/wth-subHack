      Description

      The summer heat can be terrifying and oppressive. It can test your patience and endurance in the face of rising temperatures that is not only uncomfortable, but is also unbearable. The Oster BT 150R Slim tower fan, with its silent running technology, is the perfect solution for this scenario.

      Brought to you by Osters, the renowned and trusted manufacturers of consumer electricals, the revolutionary tower fan is just what you need to beat the ravages of heat. Its space saving design makes it a perfect fit for your home, as well as your office. Equipped with the latest in modern technology, the tower fan comes with several advanced functionalities that can transform your surroundings and help you beat the heat in the height of the summer months, without breaking into a sweat. The fan can be bought online at Snapdeal at the most reasonable prices.    

       

      LED Light

      The fan body has an elegant yet robust LED panel to indicate the status of its various functions.

       

      Remote Operated

      The Oster BT 150R Slim tower fan comes equipped with a multi-functional remote that can be used to control its different features. In addition to the speed settings, you can also use the remote to access its advanced shut-off features and sleep and breeze functionality.

      Wide Air Delivery

      The bionire tower has a wide coverage, spreading its air over the widest possible area.

      Power Consumption

      With a rating of 40 w, the Oster OT16RB-IN tower fan is a silent yet highly effective solution for all your cooling needs. It acts as a major power saver, and helps you save on your electricity bills.

      Other Important Features

      The fan comes with a three speed settings. You can use these settings to select the air flow that feels perfect for you, at your convenience and according to your requirements.  The bionaire tower also features an 8 hour timer – you can use this to turn off the fan after a period of 8 hours. The sleep and breeze functionality ensures that you get a perfect and sound sleep, unhampered by noise, and lulled by a soft cool breeze. The LED display on the fan body shows which function is in use, and the multi-functional remote allows you to choose the different settings as per your requirements. The Oster BT 150R Slim tower fan is therefore a complete solution for all your cooling needs.
