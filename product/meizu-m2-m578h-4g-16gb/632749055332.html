      Meizu m2 supports dual-SIM with dual 4G. You can enjoy crystal clear voice conversation via 2G or explore high-speed web with 4G internet access. Both SIM 1 and SIM 2 support 4G network.  Additionally, the primary SIM slot can be used to support a microSD card.

      Everything looks perfect on the large 12.7 cm (5) fully laminated screen of the Meizu m2 smartphone. It offers a contrast ratio of 1000:1 and screen-to-body ratio of 75.6%. The AGC Dragontrail protective glass material prevents the display from dust and scratches.  Moreover, the IPS display technology enhances the viewing angle and ensures true-to-life colours.

      Loaded with the highly advanced 1.3 GHz Quad-core CPU with A53 architecture, Meizu m2 thrills you with a superfast processing speed. It enables you to work on multiple tabs at the same time without any time lag.
