      Description

      A display of perfection

      5" Sharp/JDI Full HD display

      You interact with Mi 4i entirely through it's stunning 5" display, which is why we added 5 different pixel-level innovations to produce clear, vivid images that look amazing from any angle and in any situation - even under bright sunlight. Using it is pure delight. We're convinced it is one of the best displays around.

      64-bit performance

      2nd gen Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 615 octa-core processor

      Push boundaries with 64-bit performance, which processes twice more data than 32-bit at any one time. The 2nd generation Snapdragon 615 is faster than previous models and has 4 performance cores clocking 1.7GHz and 4 power-saving cores running on a lower 1.1GHz. It is perfect for multitasking and running demanding applications of any kind. Paired with an Adreno 405 GPU and support for OpenGL ES 3.0, the latest standard in 3D gaming, Mi 4i is adept at handling both everyday apps and intense 3D games.

      13MP Sony/Samsung camera, f/2.0

      Intelligent two-tone flash

      Let Mi 4i inspire with the professional sharpness of its 13MP Sony/Samsung camera, artistic f/2.0 background blurring, and innovative technologies that adapt to a range of situations. So that even in low-lighting, the two-tone flash can intelligently assess room color temperature to emulate natural lighting. Selfies look amazing with a 5MP front camera and Beautify, a gorgeous built-in tool that applies one of 36 smart profiles for an all-natural glow.

       

      MIUI 6

      Well-loved by over 100 million users Now on Android L

      It's what you've been waiting for. One of the best Android operating systems, now available on Android L. MIUI has been deeply customized and is perfectly compatible with native Android games and applica-tions. Enhanced with suggestions from millions of fans worldwide, it is a living, breathing system that regularly introduces exciting new features that you'll fall in love with.

       

      4G Dual SIM,Dual Standby

      The best way to stay truly connected

      With 2G/3G/4G support on both dual SIM slots and support for up to 16 bands, there's no stopping when you're with Mi. Manage personal and work calls with ease, with intelligent dual SIM features and detailed preferred network settings for each car

      Accomplish more with a 3120mAh battery

      We've put considerable effort into fitting the highest battery capacity possible to power Mi 4i's stunning 5" display while maintaining a slim profile. This meant using the highest energy density battery technology – 4.4V 700Wh/L lithium-ion polymer cell. To save every inch of space, we then redesigned Mi 4i to include a double-sided circuit board and custom ultra-thin display. The result is an incredibly thin frame that's just 7.8mm, but with an all-day 3120mAh battery with Quick Charge that charges up to 40% in an hour.

      Been using this beauty for a week now. Totally love it.
      Hello everyone, Bought this product after an extensive research of available tons of all brands' smartphones. Totally satisfied with the performance of the phone given the price paid for it. At this price range, this is the best one anyone could get. Read so many reviews about the heating issues of Mi4i floating around majority of the tech junkie blogs and online stores. However, after moderate to heavy usage of 7 days I did not face any such issue. Perhaps, the people who were complaining got a defective piece (One of my friends bought the same phone from another online store and faced some heating issues which were subsided lately by updating the software to MiUi 7). Pros: 1. Screen/Display quality - No sane person would say anything less than awesome. 2. Battery - Easily gets you through 20-24 hours. And boost charge - Charges the phone to 60% from zero in an hour is an amazing feature. No denying the numbers claimed by Xiaomi on the paper. Usage of Performance Mode can vary the Battery Performance. But the need for Performance Mode only arises when you are playing heavy games. So balanced mode is pretty much enough for daily tasks. 3. Touch and feel: Xiaomi did an amazing job on building this product. Unlike earlier Xiaomi's phones, this one do not have a shoddy look, it feels good to hold (light weight and only 8mm thick). Easy to use even though the screen is 5". It has a special "One-Handed mode" feature (Don't know if the other phones have this too) where the screen display is scaled down to make it easy for us to use the phone with one hand. It comes in handy for people with small hands. 4. Camera: Normal and low light pictures were shot pretty awesome by the default Camera App provided by Xiaomi with MIUI. But when I used the camera function from WhatsApp to take a selfie in low light (during night in a room lit by a standard tube light), the result wasn't very satisfactory. No issues in normal light conditions though. Back camera works wonders in any condition. Cons: 1. Continuous talking for more than 1 hour over WhatsApp made the phone a bit WARM("Not a frying pan" hot as one of the frustrated buyers wrote in one of the reviews..:P) on the top side (from where we listen) 2. Only 400MB free RAM out of 2GB is available after opening 5-6 apps. It doesn't affect the performance though. UI consumes a lot of RAM to run the system. Read somewhere that Xiaomi is going to release an update to fix this and let's hope that settles the issue. Considering all, I would recommend this product to anyone who asks for my opinion and whose budget is around 13-15K.
