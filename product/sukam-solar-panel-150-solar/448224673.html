      Description

      We are living in a world which is constantly moving towards destruction by global warming, increased carbon footprint and energy crisis. The rate by which we are consuming the fossil fuels and non renewable resources of energy, we will not be able to leave any of it for our future generations. Switch to renewable sources of energy and bring home the Sukam solar panels to produce clean, green and eco friendly electrical energy. Solar power is not only environmental friendly but it will be friendly on your pocket too. Order these solar panels from Snapdeal to get the best price and best deals offered by none other in the market.

      Energy Efficiency

      The Sukam solar panels are energy efficient panels. These solar panels trap the energy from sun and harness the energy to convert it in to usable electrical energy. Ergonomic design of the solar panels prevents any loss of energy during conversion from one form to other. Energy produce by these solar panels can be easily used in residential and commercial buildings.

      Power

      The Sukam solar panels produce power of 150 watts. Maximum power voltage produced by these solar panels from Sukam is 17.7 V. Maximum current produced in these power panels is 6.8 amps. Open circuit voltage of solar panels by Sukam is 21.4 V. Power of these power panels is sufficient to light a moderate domestic building. You can run all your household appliances by the current produced by the power panels.  

      Dimensions

      Dimensions of Sukam solar panels can be bifurcated in two segments. First is dimension of the entire panel that is 110.5x65.5x3 cm. Second is the dimension of a single photo voltaic cell present in the panel. Dimension of a single cell is 100x100x.32 cm. You get 150 solar panels in a single set on ordering these solar panels online.

      Other Feature

      Sukam has brought a set of outdoor solar panels to light up your house. Install them at your terrace for trapping sunlight and producing current. High quality photo voltaic material used in these durable solar panels makes them a onetime investment. Once installed properly you will not need to look after its maintenance again and again. This makes them a set of reliable solar panels. Sukam is a trusted brand for electrical equipments. It promotes extensive research and innovation to bring the most energy efficient electrical appliances right at your door step.
