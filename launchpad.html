    Welcome to Snapdeal Launchpad.
    Innovation is at the core of Snapdeal that has helped us achieve tremendous success over the last few years. Snapdeal Launchpad aims to encourage innovators and inventors across the country and provide them a national reach for their product(s). It provides a platform for individuals/companies to share their latest innovation, and the best ones get to leverage Snapdeal's brand and reach for making their innovation a commercial success

    Our team will do the initial level of screening for all entries. All shortlisted entries will be listed in a catalog, from where customers can purchase the products. Customers will choose the winning entry through a public voting. The winning innovation will get superior visibility and will be promoted across all channels including media and site wide promotions.
    What’s in it for you?
    Distribution
    No longer do you have to scout for distributors in every geographical region. Snapdeal provides you reach to 4000+ towns & cities. Imagine working on a just-in-time model where there is no concern of making heavy working capital investments and dead inventory
    Marketing
    Companies spend years and millions of rupees building their brand and getting Top-of-mind recall in the right target segment. With Snapdeal Launchpad, this can be achieved in just a few days.

    • A catalog of all eligible products will be created. This catalog will have the products listed with their images and prices mentioned
    • Products will be arranged by their product category. Indexing on this page will be valid for 3 months from the date of listing.
    • Customers can click on individual products to read detailed description. Entrepreneurs can upload videos/images of their product on this page
    • If a customer likes the product, they can purchase it by paying online or by cash-on-delivery
    • This catalog will be promoted on the homepage of Snapdeal.com once every 2 weeks.
    • A link to this catalog will also be promoted through newsletters once every 2 weeks
    • Snapdeal will do PR communication for the catalog once every 3 months. Top performing innovations will be given special mention and testimonials taken from innovators will be published
    • Snapdeal will also share testimonials of these innovators on its social media network (Facebook/Twitter)
    • Best innovation of the quarter will be listed as Deal of the Day.
    • Apart from this, products will be listed permanently on the respective category

    FAQs
    Q. Who can participate? What qualifies as innovation?
    A. Any one and every one can participate in this. All need is to have a top class product which you believe meets an unfulfilled need of our customers.
    Q. What is the criteria for evaluation?
    A. To start with, we will screen your entry internally on a couple of parameters including innovation and marketability.
    Q. Who all does the jury constitute of?
    A. The jury would comprise of our in-house experts who come with high levels of experience in managing such associations across different product categories and third party experts, if required
    Q. What is the typical time taken from sharing and idea to starting business
    A. If you do have a registered company, this will not take more than 4 weeks.
    Q. I do not have a registered company, what do I need to do?
    A. As a policy, only registered businesses can list as sellers on Snapdeal.com. We would advise you to either get your company registered or approach a registered business who can list your innovation on Snapdeal.com on behalf of you
    Q. If I have launched my product offline previously, can I still launch it on SD launchpad?
    A. Yes you can still do.
    Q. Do I have to pay for marketing costs?
    A. No. We donot charge you for listing or marketing your innovation.
    Q. What if I do not have sufficient production capacity to fulfil the demand?
    A. At Snapdeal.com, we share intelligent analytical reports with our sellers with our recommendations on what kind of sales can they anticipate. Thus, helping them prepare and plan their inventory levels ahead of time. However we recommend you to have atleaset 10 pieces ready shipment at the time of listing
