      Any desktop computer, however feature-rich, is incomplete without a suitable monitor. A monitor is the main display unit of a desktop computer and nowadays you can take your pick from a variety of monitors like LED, LCD, HD, and more. These monitors provide excellent picture quality and are perfect for both commercial and domestic use. At Snapdeal you can choose one from a variety of brands like LG, AOC, Dell, BenQ, Samsung, ASUS and others.

      Shop Online for Monitors
      Snapdeal brings to you a wide assortment of monitors that will display bright, clear and sharp images. You can opt for a LED monitor that has a screen size of 15 or 19 inches if you want one for home use. The LED monitor price range is an affordable one, so you are sure to find something that will fit your budget and meet your requirements. Those who want monitors for office can opt for an LCD monitor that is 15 inches in size. Perfect for gamers and movie buffs, an HD monitor is an excellent choice.

      A Monitor for Everyone
      A monitor is the only display unit of a desktop computer. However, they can also be connected with laptops with the help of certain wires and cables. There are various types of monitors available, so browse an entire range available at Snapdeal. Screen size is one of the most important factors when it comes to choosing a monitor. Graphic designers, editors, photographers, animation specialists and music producers need big screens. Monitors with a screen size of 50 inches or more are good choices for professionals. Light in weight, these monitors can be mounted on a wall, just like an LED or LCD TV. Perfect for work and play, a good monitor will not strain your eyes, even if you work for hours at a stretch. Those looking for a monitor for domestic use should keep the screen resolution in mind too. A high screen resolution monitor that can be adjusted as per your requirements is a good choice. Being simple plug-and-play devices, monitors do not require any additional driver or software installation. Sleek and stylish to look at, compact monitors do not take up much desk space either.

      Over the years, bulky monitors have been replaced by slim and feature-rich monitors, some of which may even have a touchscreen. Most of them come with a manufacturer's warranty.
