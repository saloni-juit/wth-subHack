      There was a time when belts were a neglected accessory in a wardrobe. However, these are now considered to be important as these are essential for dressing up properly. These have a dual role to play as far as apparel is concerned. Not only are these functional in keeping a pair of trousers in place, but these also in add a nice touch to the overall appearance. Since a belt is worn around the waist, it creates a visual balance by distributing the appeal of the outfit to the entire body. If you are looking for belts, browse the exhaustive collection Snapdeal showcases, and take your pick.

      Shop Online for Belts at Snapdeal
      Snapdeal lists a variety of belts across categories based on type, material, buckle type, colour, size, and brand.       Buckle Up
      Be it belts for men or belts for women, this list caters to all tastes and preferences. You can choose belts based on type of material. Canvas belts are very stylish. These are easy to maintain and are available in many colours. These go well with casual wear and can be a funky accessory. These can also be worn with party wear. Leather belts have a formal or semi-formal look depending upon the design and buckle type. You will find belts with different buckle types. A pin up buckle is more traditional and has a formal appeal. Belts with auto-lock buckles look good with casuals. You may also look at belts that are reversible or single sided in design. Remember that the basic rule of wearing the correct belt is that it should complement the colour of the footwear. So choose a belt of the hue that goes with the shoes you plan to wear. For a wider option, you may also go for the combo sets that include a number of belts in different colours.
