      Throw Punches with Authority

      Explore a wide range of tough and durable boxing equipment only on Snapdeal. Items such as a Punching bags and boxing gloves are of course, a must for any boxer, but there is other equipment also that a boxer needs. Get set to bob, weave and add that much needed pop to your punches with a slick selection of boxing gear online.

      What Equipment does a Boxer Need?

      Apart from a punching bag and boxing gloves, a boxing kit comprises of many other necessities. First and foremost would be the headgear. Headgear protects your head from soft tissue damage. Always buy a quality product that protects your head and cheeks. Some of the other things that go in a boxing kit include a groin guard to protect the pelvic region, jab pads, elbow supports, and a mouth guard.

      How to Choose your Gloves?

      When you're just starting out in the arena of boxing, it is a good idea to buy training gloves or bag gloves. These have just enough padding to protect your hands while you are punching the heavy bag. Boxing gloves are designed to lessen the damage done when sparring with a partner. They are also meant to protect the hands of the boxer. It is very important that you choose the right kind of gloves, for many reasons, including safety and comfort. Gloves should give a snug fit, but they should not be too tight. If they cause your hands and fingers any discomfort, then they are not the right fit. Also, it is important that you know that leather gloves will stretch over time. Gloves come in different weights. The reason they come in different weights is because of the padding included in the glove. Heavier gloves will have more padding to protect your hands, but this will slow your punches. Vinyl gloves are not recommended for professional boxers, though they are ok for beginners and children, to use for learning. Leather gloves are better quality and also longer lasting. They also allow the hands to breathe whereas vinyl gloves hold in moisture, making palms sweaty.

      How to Choose your Punching Bag?

      A traditional punching bag or heavy bag is generally hung from a wall mounted bracket or a free-standing system. A good punching bag is made of tough-wearing leather, it's cylindrical and comes in various sizes and weights. Maize bags are teardrop shaped bags, filled with maize. Punching bags are available in a range of colours and sizes.
