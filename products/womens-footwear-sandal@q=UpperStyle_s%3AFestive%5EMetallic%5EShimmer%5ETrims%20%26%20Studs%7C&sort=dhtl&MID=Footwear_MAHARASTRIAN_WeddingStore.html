      Shop Online for Women's Sandals
      Sandals are basically a footbed attached to a designer strap. They are one of the oldest and simplest footwear styles for women. They can be paired with any eastern or fusion outfits and they help to enhance your overall appearance. You can opt for a pair of sandals based on the type or the style of the straps. You will find sandals with a back strap, a heel strap or without a back strap. The presence of back straps or heel straps make the sandals more comfortable and also add a touch of style to them. Those who do not like back straps can opt for the ones without back straps. There are also "T" strap and "V" strap sandals for you to choose from. You may also base your choice on the colour of the sandals. There are vibrant colours like green, red, black, blue, golden, yellow, peach, pink, purple and many more. If you have similar colour kurtis or tunics, you can pair them with colourful ladies' sandals.

      Choose your Heel
      You can choose a pair of sandals or slip on shoes depending upon the heel size. There are block heels, kitten heels and others that you may like to try. These heeled sandals are comfortable to wear, even for those who have never tried heels. Those who are uncomfortable wearing heels can pick flat shoes. At Snapdeal, you will be able to browse the range of sandals and shop for a pair on the basis of the upper style too. There are sandals with embroidered uppers, uppers with prints, Kolhapuri ones, solids, uppers with shimmer and embellishments and many other options.
