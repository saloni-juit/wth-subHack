      Although it is considered that DSLRs cameras are best suited for professional photographers, these cameras have three distinct levels of experience. The three levels of DSLR cameras are beginners, mid level and professional. Beginner DSLR cameras are for those who have recently taken up photography, and mid level DSLR cameras are for those who have outgrown their entry level DSLRs. Professional cameras are for seasoned photographers. Photographers can experiment with DSLR cameras as they have the option of changing lenses according to their requirement.
