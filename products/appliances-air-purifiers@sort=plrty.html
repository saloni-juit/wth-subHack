People usually have a misconception that pollution exists in outdoors only. However, the air is as polluted indoors as it is outdoors.

      Now the question is how to make your home and office (where you spend so many hours daily) safe?
      Well, you cannot control the quantity of dust, pollen, and other allergens in the air outside. But, you can surely remove it inside using an air purifier. Air Purifier is a device that can be used to remove contaminants from the air in a room. As this device reduces the concentration of contaminants in a room, it is considered to be beneficial for allergy sufferers and asthmatics too.
