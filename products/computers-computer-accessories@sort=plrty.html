      A computer needs a range of accessories and peripherals to function properly. Keyboard protectors, mouse pads, extension cords, cables and plugs, and others are some of these accessories. To find all that you need, under one roof, browse the collection available at Snapdeal.

      Shop Online for Computer Accessories
      Of all computer accessories, an extension cord is one of the important ones. A computer has a lot of parts that require separate sources of power and this gives rise to the need for multiple power outlets. Since more than two or three adjacent plug points are not always available, it is feasible to opt for extension cords. Another major advantage of these extension cords is that they also protect the monitor and the CPU from voltage fluctuations. There are 3, 4, 6 and even 8 socket extension cords available at Snapdeal, so opt for the one that is compatible with your computer. You can also take your pick from international plugs, power AC adapters and more. All these products are required to power your computer. To protect the keyboard of your laptop or desktop, take a look at the collection of keyboard protectors. These keyboard protectors are easy to use, and will keep your keyboard dust-free. You will also find multiple mouse pads. Those who make a lot of presentations or like watching films the old fashioned way may opt for a projector with a ceiling mount.

      For the Desktop and the Laptop
      Computer accessories are useful for both desktop computers and laptops. Products like USB hubs that allow multiple USB devices to connect to your desktop or laptop, are convenient. To read and browse the contents of CDs and DVDs, opt for a DVD burner from brands like ASUS, LG, Lenovo and others. Blank media refers to blank DVDs that you can use to store and transfer files. You may opt for a pack of 50 recordable DVDs from Snapdeal. To make your laptop look all the more classy, choose trendy laptop skins. These are easy to attach and detach as well. Cleaning kits are also available here and they help you to maintain your device. A card reader is another important accessory to order. The HDMI cable price range is also quite affordable and thus you can pick up one of those too.

      Most of the products available are accessories from reputed brands and they also come with a manufacturer's warranty. These products will last you a long time and you will not have to worry about after-sales support either.
