      Taking a cold shower is not something pleasant for both the kids and the elderly people. Similarly, sleeping at night under blankets and still shivering is not a comfortable experience either. To solve these problems, Snapdeal brings to you a wide variety of geysers and room heaters that will keep you warm and cosy.

      Shop Online for Geysers and Heaters
      Easy to install and very user-friendly, geysers can be used by people across most age groups. When you browse Snapdeal, you will find that there are two options available when it comes to choosing a product to heat your bath water. You may opt for either a geyser or an immersion rod. Immersion rods are convenient, portable and do not require any installation process. To heat water, all you need to do is immerse the device in a metal bucket full of water and switch on the power. Bajaj, Khaitan, Gryphon and other reputed brands offer immersion rods of various capacities and designs. An instant geyser is another option you can look at. A small, compact geyser has minimal storage capacity and heats the water as and when it passes through the device. Thus you need not switch on the geyser and wait for the water to heat up. A storage water heater is an option that might interest you, especially if you have a large family and a big bathroom. All these products will grant you access to hot water at any time during the day or night.

      Keep your rooms cosy
      A wide range of room hearters is available here that would let you heat up your room. The major advantage of a room heater is that it does not take up much space and are quite easy to use. Within a few minutes of switching on a room heater, the temperature of any cold room will increase. Snapdeal brings to you a variety of room heaters for you to choose from. You can opt for oil filled radiators or the regular ones. The range of oil filled heaters available here have a capacity below 6 litres and thus are prefect for small rooms. All the heaters are light in weight, are highly mobile and can be easily moved from one room to another. Opt for brands like Orpat, Bajaj, and Maharaja for room heaters that are not only effective, but also good to look at.

      The water and room heaters available here are products from reputed brands and also come with a manufacturer's warranty.
