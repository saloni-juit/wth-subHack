      Micromax
      Micromax Informatics Limited is a rapidly growing Indian consumer electronics company. It is one of the largest consumer electronics company in India and has been ranked as the 10th largest mobile phone manufacturer in the world.

      Micromax mobiles are loaded with features such as a good battery backup, QWERTY keypads, Dual SIM Dual Standby phones, Quad Core budget smart phones, etc. In the recent past the company launched its very successful Canvas range of phones, as well as its first waterproof model - the X205. Today, Micromax offers an exhaustive range of 3G phones including the Micromax Canvas 2 A110, Micromax Superfone Ninja-A50, Micromax A116 Canvas HD, Micromax Superfone Punk A44 and Micromax Superfone A1017 among others. 3G phones have features such as touch screens, QWERTY keypads, FM radios, dual SIM card slots, music players, and much more.

      Micromax has successfully made an indelible mark in the smartphone category and has now set its sights on the tablet market. It boasts of three advanced tablets namely the Funbook P280, Funbook Ultra P580, and the Canvas Tab P650E. These tablets are more affordable as compared to the devices sold by its competitors. The company has also made successful inroads in the data cards, routers, modems, home theatre systems, mobile cases, LED televisions, audio, video, computers and peripherals segments. All these products are available for sale on the Micromax online store.

      The brand now has its presence felt across India, as well as the world. Sold in Russia and SAARC markets, consumers can buy 3G mobile phones and other products around the globe through their Micromax online store. They are available in all the leading online shopping portals.

      The company aims to provide affordable product offerings. It believes that innovation and technology should not come at a high price.
